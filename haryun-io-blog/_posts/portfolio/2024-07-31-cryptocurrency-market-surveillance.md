Developed a **real-time monitoring system** for suspicious virtual-asset transactions to comply with the **Virtual Asset User Protection Act** and the regulations/guidance of the **FSS & FSC**.

<!--more-->

## Project Overview

This project focused on detecting abnormal and potentially unfair trading patterns in near real-time, backed by scalable data processing and reproducible reporting for regulatory responses.
